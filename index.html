About Bent Back and Beyond

Melon has been tattooing since 2004 all across the United States but landed in Asheville in the spring of 2022. He enjoys doing many styles of tattooing, including dark fantasy and horror, color realism, stylized wood cut and stippling as well as american traditional. He has always worked with his clients to assure that the tattoo they want becomes the tattoo they truly deserve. Whether you are looking for a small piece or a full back, Melon works with you to make sure your tattoo is exactly what you want. Reach out today to book your consultation!
